Experimental: Jan 2018 | Jun 2020

Working on the short film "Frenesí" as co-writer, co-director, director of photography, and co-art director has been a significant challenge and an enriching experience. Together with audiovisual creator Catalina Millán Rebaudi, I learned a lot about technical aspects, but the most valuable lessons came from teamwork and leadership. This experience taught me the importance of good communication and active listening, recognizing that each team member contributes in their own way, leading to a more productive development. I deeply value every step of this process, as it contributes to my personal and professional growth.
